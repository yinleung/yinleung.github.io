About

I am currently a PhD student at The Institute of Statistical Mathematics, and The Graduate University for Advanced Studies, SOKENDAI, supervised by Prof. Han Bao.

Previously, I received my M.Eng. in Computer Technology at Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I worked on the research topic of signal processing and localization under the supervision of Prof. Sheng Xu. Before that, I received my B.Sc. in Physics at Sun Yat-sen University.

Research interests: Deep Learning Theory, Optimization.
